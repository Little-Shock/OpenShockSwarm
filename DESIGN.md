# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Theme Overview

Our theme is designed with a **light** color mode, focusing on vibrancy and clear communication. The foundational color is a bright yellow, complemented by a rich purple and a lively green.

## Color Palette

The color palette is built around specific hex values to maintain brand consistency.

* **Primary Color:** `#FFD500` (A vibrant yellow, serving as the main brand accent and for primary calls to action.)
* **Secondary Color:** `#A855F7` (A rich purple, used for secondary elements and complementary information.)
* **Tertiary Color:** `#22C55E` (A bright green, utilized for highlights, success states, or additional accents.)
* **Neutral Color:** `#000000` (A deep black, providing strong contrast for text and structural elements.)

## Typography

Our typography system is chosen for readability and distinctiveness across different text roles.

* **Headlines:** `spaceGrotesk` (Used for all major headings to provide a modern and impactful feel.)
* **Body Text:** `inter` (Selected for its excellent readability, ideal for paragraphs and longer content blocks.)
* **Labels:** `spaceGrotesk` (Applied to labels and UI elements for consistency with headline typography.)

## Shape and Form

The visual language incorporates a moderate level of roundedness for UI elements, balancing modern aesthetics with a friendly feel.

* **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate rounding, providing soft corners without being overly pill-shaped.)

## Spacing

The spacing within the UI is set to a normal density, ensuring clarity and comfortable information consumption.

* **Spacing:** `2` (Normal spacing, providing a balanced layout for content and components.)
